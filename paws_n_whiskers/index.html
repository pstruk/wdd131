Pet Care Tips

Summer has arrived, but there are a few things that you should consider, if you're heading to the lake with your dog. Some dogs are natural-born swimmers, but others may not like water at all. If you want everyone to have a great experience, there are some things that you can do to help your dog. If in doubt about your pet's ability near water, then we recommend getting them a dog life-jacket.

Introduce them slowly into small bodies of water, perhaps a kiddie pool with a few toys. Don't forget to keep some treats on hand as a reward. Keep them leashed to maintain their safety and allow them to enter the water slowly, at their own pace. If they're hesitant, take a few steps into the water yourself to show them that it's not so scary. Never toss, push, or pull your dog into the water, as it is dangerous and likely to increase their fear. A little patience goes a long way and before you know it, they'll be begging YOU to go to the beach!
