Adoption Considerations

  1. Pets are a lifetime commitment; most cats, dogs, and rabbits live 10-16 years. Are you ready?
  2. Does your household have children or elderly members that your pet needs to get along with?
  3. Are you ready to deal with pet hair on a daily basis? There will be shedding.
  4. Is anyone in your household allergic to animals?
  5. Are you ready for the financial commitment? Pets need food, grooming, and vet care.
  6. Who will carry the responsibility for your pet's care? Will they be home during the day?
  7. Do you have the equipment? All adopted cats/rabbits must be transported home in a secure carrier. Dogs must be leashed and collared.
